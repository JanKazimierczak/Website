About

Engineering Science student at the University of Toronto, focused on aerospace engineering.

Background

Who I Am

First-year Engineering Science student with a 3.63 GPA, focused on aerospace engineering and quantitative analysis. Member of UTAT (University of Toronto Aerospace Team), actively developing CAD skills and exploring international aerospace opportunities.

My interests span spacecraft propulsion systems, structural analysis, parametric design, and physics simulations. I'm driven by mathematical rigour and applying engineering principles to solve real-world challenges.

Where I'm Headed

Currently exploring aerospace internship opportunities in Europe and considering advanced studies at institutions like TU Delft. I'm particularly drawn to liquid propulsion research and the systems-level thinking required to design reliable, high-performance spacecraft.

Outside of coursework I'm developing hands-on experience through UTAT's liquid propulsion division — working on real hardware alongside driven peers.
