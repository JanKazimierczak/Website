Project

Bridge structural analysis

This page focuses on the part of the bridge project that I find most valuable: translating structural ideas into calculations, assumptions, and expected failure behavior that can actually be checked.

Analytical emphasis

Why this project matters to me

Bridge work appeals to me because it sits at the intersection of physical intuition and disciplined analysis. It requires understanding load paths, identifying weak points, and deciding whether the structure is genuinely credible rather than only visually convincing.

Inputs

Geometry, material limits, loading assumptions, and support conditions.

Methods

Structural reasoning, numerical checks, and performance comparisons.

Outputs

Predicted behavior, weak points, and design implications.
